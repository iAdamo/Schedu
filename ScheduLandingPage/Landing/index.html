Student Information System (SIS)

  • Manage all users in one system: Students, parents, teachers and admin staff
  • Data migration service included to import all students and parents using excel sheet
  • Gain 360° view of a student on the Student Profile page that displays personal information and auto-captures activity and lesson history, deposits, invoices and payments
  • Set user access rights and privileges across a single or multi-outlet setup
  • Auto-generate logins for Student and Parent Portals to show respective lessons, invoices, attendance and reports

Learn About SchEdu

The Schedule School Management System isn't just a portfolio project; it's a collective desire to revolutionize the way schools operate. As a team, we've felt the frustrations of data mismanagement, sky-high administrative costs, and the tediousness of tasks like slow registrations process and misplaced files. That's why we've come together with a shared vision: to create a solution that transforms the educational sector.

Imagine a registration process so smooth, it's practically effortless. Say goodbye to hunting down misplaced files and endless document queries; our system keeps everything tidy and accessible. And forget fumbling with cash during payments—we streamline transactions for added security and speed.

Our passion for efficiency and commitment to excellence drive us forward. We're not just building software; we're crafting a tool that empowers schools to focus on what truly matters: education.

As this is just the beginning of our inspiration, join us on this journey as we pave the way for a future where administrative burdens are a thing of the past, and educational endeavors flourish like never before.
